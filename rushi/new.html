// Architecture

Six engines.
One platform.

Each engine is purpose-built for a specific audio intelligence task. Together, they power the most complete voice platform ever built.

01
VXR-Core
The synthesis brain. Text goes in, studio-grade speech comes out. Powers all four generation modes with sub-second latency.
02
VXR-Native
Regional language pipeline handling 12 languages with native pronunciation — authentic intonation, not robotic transliteration.
03
VXR-Replica
Voice cloning engine. Upload a 30-second sample, get a digital twin. Generate speech in that voice across all 12 languages.
04
VXR-Scribe
Transcription engine. Audio goes in, text comes out. Includes speaker diarization, word-level timestamps, and subtitle export.
05
VXR-Omnix
Language intelligence layer. Auto-detects Hinglish, converts Romanized text, handles numbers, currency, abbreviations, dates, and pauses.
06
VXR-Master
8-step studio mastering pipeline. Denoise, normalize, compress, EQ, breath smooth, artifact removal. Quality grades A through F.
